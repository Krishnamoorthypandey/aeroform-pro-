HOW TO USE THIS TOOL — READ BEFORE STARTING
1
Select Metal Type
Choose the metal family (Aluminum, Stainless, Titanium, Carbon Steel, Nickel, Copper) from the left sidebar, then the exact alloy and temper.
2
Enter Part Geometry
Enter thickness (t), inner bend radius (R), bend angle, flange A, flange B, part length, and number of bends in the sidebar.
3
Read Results Tab
See bend allowance, flat blank length, springback, and die/punch recommendations instantly. Red warnings in the bottom bar = action required.
4
Check Tooling Tab
Get specific die type (V-die / Roll die / Wing die / Wipe die), punch nose radius, V-die opening, and press tonnage check.
5
Compliance Tab
Pass/Fail checks for MBR, flange length, press tonnage, grain direction, angle tolerance, and non-linear zone analysis — all in one place.
6
AI Vision (Optional)
Upload your 2D drawing, loft view, formed part photo, or 3D screenshot. AI identifies geometry, recommends die/punch, and flags risks. Needs internet only for this tab.
📱 Mobile: Tap the orange "INPUTS" button (top right) to open the input panel. All tabs are in the top navigation bar.
💡 Offline: All calculations work without internet. Only AI Vision needs internet.
📐 Grain direction: Always bend across the grain (⊥) for aerospace — tighter radii, less cracking risk.
🌡 Springback: The punch angle shown is what you SET on the machine — not the part angle. Always trial-bend on same batch first.
⚠ Long parts: For parts >500mm, go to Alternatives tab — there are specific recommendations for long-part challenges like roll die vs wing die limitations.
